Classical Studies

Ability is what you're capable of doing!

The study of Latin and Greek opens a window into the roots of Western civilization and the origins of modern language. As a Classical Studies student at EIHE, you'll study great authors like Homer and Socrates, learn some of the world's most ancient languages, receive a broad perspective on classical culture and prepare to thrive in any career.
