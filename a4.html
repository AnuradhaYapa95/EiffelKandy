Sociology

Challenge your limits!

Sociology is a social science which helps to make sense of the way society works. Looking at life through a lens of inequality, difference and diversity, sociology at our university brings real issues into sharp focus. Supported by a nationally renowned academic team, you'll explore how topics such as class, ethnicity, food, gender, health, media and poverty shape our lives and experiences. Choose among the plethora of career options such as Guidance Counselor, Lawyer, Policy Analyst, PR Specialist, Management Consultant...etc by following and successfully completing this 3 year degree program.
