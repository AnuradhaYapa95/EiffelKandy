Marketing Management

Your big success is only one click away....

Marketing is a dynamic business function which links a business to the marketplace. It's much more than just advertising or selling. This degree course aims to develop the students understanding of how both Business and Marketing Managers function. Marketing management is regularly faced with the need to analyze complex situations, develop a viable plan of action and pursue this plan to successful completion. This course focuses on practice as well as theory.
